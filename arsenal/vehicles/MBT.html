MTA5 "RHINO"

UNITED HOLLOW REPUBLIC — Main Battle Tank

Overview

The MTA5 "RHINO" serves as the United Hollow Republic’s premier main battle tank, representing a new generation of armored warfare built around mobility, survivability, and networked lethality. Drawing design inspiration from next-generation western MBT concepts, the Rhino integrates advanced automation and reduced crew requirements while maintaining overwhelming firepower on the battlefield.

The platform features a partially unmanned turret configuration, relocating crew into a heavily protected armored capsule within the hull. This layout significantly improves survivability while enabling more compact turret geometry and reduced target profile. An advanced autoloader system ensures consistent firing rates and allows for expanded ammunition handling capabilities.

At its core, the Rhino utilizes a hybrid propulsion system, combining a high-output multi-fuel engine with electric drive assistance. This enables rapid acceleration, silent maneuvering at low speeds, and reduced thermal signature during reconnaissance or ambush operations.

The vehicle is deeply integrated into the Republic’s battlefield network, capable of sharing targeting data, coordinating with drones, and acting as a command node for armored formations. Combined with its layered armor, active protection systems, and adaptive countermeasures, the MTA5 is designed to dominate both conventional and near-peer combat environments.
