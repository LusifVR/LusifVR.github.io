WEAPONS

UHR SERVICE WEAPONS

The current weapons in use by UHR

RA-7X

6.8×51mm NTT

Manufacturer: ARNEL

The next generation service rifle for the Republic Military. Trading capacity for velocity in caliber size, this rifle was an advocated replacement for the RM4 and offered to service for select units.

RT-18

5.56x45mm NTT

Manufacturer: ARNEL & AWMG

A primary service rifle with next-generation upgrades in mind. Commonly used by the Republic's Special Forces Teams, ARNEL and AWMG were contracted in a joint project to produce a median-level rifle.

RA-52

.338 (8.6mm) NTT

Manufacturer: ARNEL

The next generation service machine-gun used by the Republic Military. With its rapid success in deployment proving the capabilities, it became the service squad weapon for the Republic.

RA-12

6.5 SUPER NTT

Manufacturer: ARNEL

Long-range engagement rifle suited for marksman roles while still withholding an ample rate of fire for close-range capabilities

R4C-45

.45 AUTO NTT

Manufacturer: AWMG

A well-rounded and low-recoil sub machine gun. Commonly issued to vehicle crews or embassy personnel, its compact size makes it a weapon of choice for rapid and close units.

R-872

12G BUCKSHOT

Manufacturer: AWMG

A light-weight pump-action shotgun for engineering or logistical operations teams in purpose for close quarters combat or breaching. While not entirely conventional, its the weapon of choice for internal use and solving problems.

M17 TK

9x19mm NTT

Manufacturer: ARNEL

The standard service pistol for the Republic. Appearing in two different variants, the modularity of the handgun made it popular for many units across the republic.

LK 3

9x19mm NTT

Manufacturer: HACKLOCKE

A simple and robust handgun imported from HACKLOCKE commonly used by law enforcement or logistical units. The magazines can be inter-changed from service pistols.

R45

.45 AUTO NTT

Manufacturer: AWMG

A slimmed handgun boasting the 45 AUTO round, trading magazine capacity for punch power. With an integrated compensator and low-bearing spring makes recoil minimal.

▔▔▔▔▔▔ SUPPORT WEAPONS ▔▔▔▔▔▔

The various field pickup weaponry available to ground units.

M3 Anti-Tank Launcher

84mm AT

The M3 Launcher is a shoulder-fired recoilless rifle designed to deal with armored targets, fortified positions, and obstacle removal. It's advanced computertronic optics allows troops to accurately designate targets.

AT6 Disposable

84mm AT

The AT6 is a lightweight, infantry-based, disposable launcher. Firing the same round as the M3, its ease 'free-fire' and disposable design makes it an effective quick anti-tank weapon.

M84A1 AMR

12.7mm HE NTT

The M84A1 Anti-Material-Rifle is a long-range precision rifle used to destroy enemy equipment, vehicles, or armored targets. It's high explosive round makes it extremely effective at disabling enemy remote-turrets or optics.

The various devices and tactical equipment in use by UHR

SOFLAM Designator

IR LASER MARKER

An Infrared laser designator to mark targets for aircraft or support elements. It can create its own infrared radio signal, allowing radar-guided munitions to lock onto its marker.

IR Beacon

INFARED MARKER

An Infrared marker to mark positions and designate locations based as a single-point light. Useful to act as static marking points or signify friendly fortifications.

Hornet Drone

Fast Recon Drone

Remotely operated recon drone sporting an advanced stabilization and tracking suite, making it a suitable use for forward-observing units.
