Gameplay Changes

Added 'WARFARE' mode. WARFARE will represent a majority of the remainder of Phase 7. Utilizing long-term campaign actions as well as extended duration operations. This will include a Zulu-post mode as well as branching paths as previously voted on in Cmd-comms. AI Improvements and tweaks > All systems related to AI, for Infantry & Vehicles, have been improved. This includes additional logic for ambushes, selective targeting, more realistic sightlines, and more. Vehicle Response > AI Vehicles, specifically friendly AI vehicles, can respond to friendly actions, such as advancing with players, firing on shared targets, or sharing datalink information.

Vehicles – Improvements

Overall improved handling and suspension, including synced performance to remote players, especially those with slower connections. Various utility systems now feature complete. This includes screens such as engine display, status page, fuel tank readout, drivetrain view, and many others. Screens will now only show "FAULT" if there is an error or a module is missing.

Electronic Warfare

Introducing the final interactivity addition for vehicles. EW and ECM. Armored Vehicles, such as the Rhino MTA3 and Marshal ACV-2 have been outfitted with both softkill ECM as well as hardkill APS. The commander seat has access to both of these systems and will receive a warning alarm when a threat or projectile is inbound. This system is not modeled for the gunner or driver and requires a commander to utilize. Squad vehicles, such as the Hunter JLTV and Bison APC also have an advanced active protective system. They utilize the same controls as the Rhino and Marshal commander seat so additional training is not necessary.

Improved Logistics

Expanding Logistic library from 20 objects to over 100 objects, improving stability, adding towing and trailer options, and more. Friendly Infantry AI will now deploy to established FOBs. When a FOB is constructed, friendly AI units will spawn to protect it. This allows for the players to continue pushing onto an objective, establishing a 'leapfrogging' gameplay method. This also means that Enemy AI can spawn at Enemy FOBs. This will require players to be more selective with positioning, and have more notable movement. Enemy & Friendly support systems will fire on opposing team fortifications if within range.

Additional Taskings

During the first half of Phase 7 we ran our first few test Tasking missions which all recieved positive feedback. Additional bonus taskings and any taskings missed throughout Phase 6 and the first half of Phase 7 will be awarded available! Taskings will be posted to the campaign channel when available.
