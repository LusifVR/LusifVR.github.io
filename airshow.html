HAVENPORT

Havenport is an expansive exploration map located in the Southern Aspin region. The island features hidden locations, interactive activities, coastal towns, beach-side vacations, and aviation infrastructure designed for combined air operations.

The environment includes a complete operational airfield, mountain flyovers, beaches, urban districts, and open ocean patrol sectors for nice visuals, and an explorable map.

During the event, guests will witness live demos, flyovers, weapons demonstrations, formation flights, logistics operations, and post-show static displays hosted throughout the island complex.
