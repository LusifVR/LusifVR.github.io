Who We Are  

The Russo-Japanese Federation is a community dedicated to military simulation and roleplay, primarily within VRChat and Arma3, as well as a smaller amount within Star Citizen. The original community was created May 9th 2018. The stories told in the community operations revolve around a Russo/Japanese near Future/Neo setting, driven by player choice, all of which started with the FRTM back in 2018, (Federation of Rossiyskaya of the Transcontinental Mountains) however, due to it's massively developing story and the way the members have steered it, it came to be known as the JSKD (Japanese Shiroi Kitsune Dynasty), then further evolved into the RJPF (Russo Japanese Plurinational Federation) after a drastic lore-changing operation occurred. The community was founded on high moral standards of integrity, self discipline and respect.

About Us

Our Mission

RJPF is a premier military simulation community, offering a unique blend of immersive role-playing experiences in a future/neo Russo/Japanese theme. Explore the depths of our dynamic narrative and engage with a community dedicated to realism and creativity.

Since our inception in 2018, we have been evolving our storyline, providing members with custom avatars, positions, and tasks. Experience our rich lore through active mission worlds and maps, and craft your own character to be part of our dynamic narrative.
