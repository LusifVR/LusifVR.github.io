UNITED HOLLOW REPUBLIC [UHR]

Unified in 2018, the 3 nations of Aspin, Paraq, and Poro, established a mutual defense agreement [The North Tepix Treaty] that would combine their governments into a single entity. With common historical roots of the 'Hollows', a seafaring people in the region, citizens of all 3 nations shared mutual ancestry and values. With a strong sense of pride and patriotism, the Republic is formed of the combined militaries of the 3 nations that founded. A strong Naval presence combined with a well-maintained Logistical assault ground force makes the Republic an extremely effective and formidable fighting force.

IMPERIAL DIRECTIVE ALLIANCE [IDA]

An Imperial narrative founded in the wake of the second ITF war. Many individuals tired of the Republic's reach and strong anti-hollow values formed a new alliance, that would finally unite many of the disassociated factions south of the equator. Despite a strong southern presence, the IDA focuses on resource and capability expansion. With extremely high manpower numbers, and multiple avenues of expanding technological advancements, makes the IDA an extremely influential force in any region in enters as well as a 1:1 adversary to the Republic.

BRACHOVISK LIBERATION FRONT [BLF]

Following the host nation's second collapse- what was left of the Chovik Union fled to the south and formed a new type of governing body that would focus on regaining a foothold on their country. Despite over a decade of reunification attempts, most of the country lays waste to contractors in search of a rare earth mineral as well as thousands of scattered documents unveiling some of the world's dirtiest secrets. While they have a strong civilian-militia backed force and foreign support, the Liberation Forces have a constant power vacuum, leading to their individual alignment clouded.

USTALINA ARMY [UA]

Ustal is a smaller nation of a dense population of old-world values. While the Ustalina Army lacks in the latest technologies, they make up for it in sheer numbers. Their population has an extremely strong sense of self pride and individualism that causes their government to take advantage, seiging power and control in the region. Due to the proximity to ocean pathways, many of the nation's income comes from providing travel ports and visas to international shipping boats, taxing the routes heavily to turn a profit passing through their waters.

JINSAN RONIN FORCES [JRF]

Following its liberation from the ITF in 2031, Jinsan reestablished its military in a new protective state, closing off from the rest of the world in a traditional-style isolationist government. While many of its military and governing styles follow a 'Defensive Posture Only' status- Jinsan has participated in many incursions over the last few decades that were within close proximity of its home. Despite a smaller military and a short-range power projection capability, Jisan's force is deeply routed in drawing the enemy to them- in which they will accept nothing but total victory.

INTERNATIONAL TRADE FEDERATION [ITF]

A governmental take over of the Chivan peoples in the early 2000s forced its military to abide by their leaders and established a new Federation focused on region and resource control. Using military power and propaganda, the ITF captured a large sum of the surrounding nations and territories, promising a new peaceful world era. Their true intentions of military and economic expansion would be revealed when they tested the world's first atom bomb on the nation of ETO, marking the start of their collapse.

RILAN ARMED FORCES [RAF]

Formed from a deep pocket of aristocrat rulers, the Rilan Armed Forces expands most of its economy into weapons and research development in order to stay on top of surrounding nations in the region. Despite a smaller population, most of Rilan's military comprises of surrounding neighboring nation volunteers that are paid well to fight on Rila's behalf. Despite this, Rilan's own citizens- while rarely deployed, are some of the world's finest fighting troops, specializing in rapid, raid-focused campaigns.

ALLIED ATUEX FORCES [AAF]

A multi-national fighting force established out of the concern of a new-world uprising. Its fighters consist of many volunteers as well as mainland fighters from the country of Akieyna, it would assist in the takeover of the powershifting Strymia. Despite Strymia's eventual stabilization, the AAF was left betrayed when many of its fighters turned on Akieyna officials, leaving the faction in a broken, scattered state.

ALLIED SUHAN EMPIRE ARMY [ASEA]

Following the collapse of the International Trade Federation, surviving leaders escaped to northern capitals and separated themselves from the rest of Chiva. Refusing aid or alteration to the collapsed federation, the Suhan Empire maintained all remaining resources and manpower, establishing a walled border between ASEA and the rest of Chiva, focusing on their own survival. Its current state is closed off to the rest of the world and while quiet, many of the recent arms trade and war developments have been attributed to ASEA.

CHERNA DEFENSE ARMY [CDA]

Cherna's stability has been questioned for decades due to its nature in dealing with black-market trades, international PMC dealings, and un-tapped mineral deposits. A smaller group of domestic and foreign volunteers formed the CDA in order to gain a foothold and establish security in the region. While results are slow, they are steady and have brought hope to many of the people who still live in the Cherna region.

VERDANT PACT [VP]

The Verdant Pact is a private military corporation founded in 2054 following the destruction of the Hannibal Site. When a group of internal VIPs were killed in the strike, Including Forrin Govansky, the funding was moved to the highest bidder. That being Darijn Govansky, Forrin's son. Out of distrust for security surrounding Hannibal, including Rilan forces, he established a new force of personnel dedicated to eliminating anyone in his way of Verdant's projects.

NANTA CORPORATION [NANTA]

A private paramilitary hired by the NANTA foundation based out of Aspin. With a high amount of hired guns and equipment, it transports, manufactures, and protects high-value assets and resources. During a recent incident involving NANTA and unrefined N28, its origin and internal buyers were placed under investigation. The remaining paramilitary units struck again at the southern coast of Paraq- more formally stating their standpoint against the UHR.

Once a benefactor to the Republic, now one of its greatest threats.
