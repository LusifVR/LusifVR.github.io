WELCOME TO UHR

The United Hollow Republic [UHR] is a military-simulation (milsim)
community that hosts events and operations within virtual-reality.
Using a player-guided story, members partake in an ongoing campaign
and experience simulated combat scenarios with combined arms & logistics
built on realism and unit cohesion.

UHR strives forward in bringing the most immersive experience and scenarios
you won't find anywhere else!

We are an extremely active community with Operations/Milsim events,
Trainings every week, Movie Nights, Competitions, Game Nights, and more!

Within UHR, content is ever evolving. Each week updates are made to content
to keep things in a constant flow. Constantly improving and pushing the bar,

UHR is the proud spearhead of Milsim VR!
