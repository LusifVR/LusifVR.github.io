-=- UHR UNITS -=-

GROUND FORCES [RGF]

COMBAT INFANTRY

[RGF-INF]


THE INFANTRY OF THE REPUBLIC IS THE BACKBONE OF THE FORCE. MAIN OPERATIVES WHO CARRY OUT COMBAT MISSIONS DURING WARTIME CAMPAIGNS. 

SPECIAL RECONNAISSANCE

[RGF-RCN]

UHR'S RECON IS A LIGHT, FORWARD UNIT THAT PARTAKES ACTION IN GATHERING INTELLIGENCE, EARLY ADVANCEMENTS, AND LOGISTICAL MAPPING. 

ARMORED BATTLE GROUP

[RGF-AMR]

RGF's ARMORED GROUP IS RESPONSIBLE FOR GROUND SUPPORT FOR THE REST OF THE FORCE. FROM INFANTRY FIGHTING VEHICLES TO MAIN BATTLE TANKS, AMR BRINGS METAL.

MILITARY POLICE

[RGF-LSO]

THE REPUBLIC MILITARY POLICE IS RESPONSIBLE FOR THE PROTECTION AND SECURITY OF MILITARY BASES, CIVIL UNREST, AND HOMELAND ENFORCEMENT.

COMBAT ENGINEERING GROUP

[RGF-ENG]

RGF COMBAT ENGINEERS ARE THE PROBLEM SOLVERS OF THE GROUND FORCES. RESPONSIBLE FOR REPAIR OR DEMOLITION, THE RGF-ENG CONTROL THE BATTLEFIELD.

COMBAT AVIATION WING

[RGF-AVW]

AVW UNIT OF THE GROUND FORCES ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR AVIATION WITHIN RGF. CREW-CHIEFS, GUNNERS, OR PILOTS, THE AVIATION WING HANDLE AIRCRAFT WITHIN THE GROUND FORCES.

MARITIME CORPS [RMC]

FIGHTER WING

[RMC-CFP]

FIGHTER WING SQUADRONS ARE THE AVIATION DIRECT-ACTION AGAINST ENEMY AIRCRAFT. SPECIALIZING IN MARITIME-AIR PATROL, RMC-CFP MAKE UP MULTIPLE A/A AND A/G SQUADRONS.

ATTACK WING

[RMC-AXE]

ATTACK WING SQUADRONS MAKE UP THE MAJORITY OF ATTACK AIRCRAFT. THESE SQUADRONS SPECIALIZE IN AIR-TO-GROUND CAS AND DIRECT ACTION FLIGHT.

UTILITY WING

[RMC-CUP]

UTILITY WING SQUADRONS ARE AIRCRAFT FLOWN FOR TRANSPORT, CARGO, AWACS, AND OTHER LOGISTICAL ELEMENTS. SOME MAY CARRY ARMAMENTS BUT ARE NOT DIRECT ACTION.

VESSEL FLEET

[RMC-VFT]

VESSEL FLEET TROOPERS AND THEIR RESPECTIVE UNITS ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR MAINTAING AND DEFENDING MARITIME CORPS SHIPS. THE BROAD ROLE CAN BE CATEGORIZED FROM FLIGHT DECK CREW TO SPECIALIZED DEFENSE TEAMS.
